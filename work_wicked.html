Carbon footprint Challenge

Hi-fi visual prototype for an app that could to keep track of carbon footprint and encourage you to take action to save the World.

Concept development / Design thinking / UI design

Overview

For this assignment we were working with Wicked problem - Environmental threats. We had to come up with a concept that could be solved with digital media. From conducted research we decided to focus on: “ Awareness and individual impact on the environment - how can we make the environment more personal and how can we show people how they impact the nature with their behavior?”.

TIME

1.5 weeks

ROLE

UI designer, Concept development

CASE

Concept development with digital solution.

Step 01

Empathise

We started by researching the environment problem and how people relate to it. Combining research and personal experiences we came up with a hypothesis -" People are aware of the threats, but lose interest in protecting the environment as they are not able to see how they individually impact the environment and what action to take".

Step 03

Ideate

We worked with the Design Thinking method and came up with several questions we had to answer to come up with a full concept, and we did that using the “Idea String”. We wrote all ideas, silly or good, on sticky notes. Afterward, we read through all of the ideas and tried to combine them, looking for the best possible answers to our questions.

Step 05

IA and sketches

The goal was to make a user-friendly app with clear navigation, therefore, we used card sorting to categorize the content. After IA was done we began to create the layout. As wanted to implement swiping function to make the journey pleasant we came up with a design that would support the vertical and horizontal swiping.

Step 02

Focus group interview

The hypothesis was confirmed and additionally, we found out that people are more likely to do something if they see their friends being active. Also, they like to follow trending challenges and competitions and they want to be reminded about saving the resources and money.

Step 04

Pretotyping

As we were working with a concept first, product second mindset, we had to find out if users are interested in our solution - app. We created a short pitch to test the idea, gain feedback and more ideas on what functionalities and options the app should include and what would motivate to use the product.

Step 06

Mockups

When the sketches were done, we create high fidelity prototypes in terms of design. These prototypes are functional to some extent demonstrating the functionality and transition. We worked with design trends, emphasizing the visual experience with HD images and added gesture suggestions to help with interaction.

Final prototype

We create a prototype for an app that would encourage people to take action in order to help the environment. This would be achieved with different challenges where users could compete with friends to see who leaves the less carbon footprint. When participating in challenges the user would get notifications or tips based on their daily routine to help them succeed. In addition to that, the user would be able to connect to their utility providers and see how much energy and water they use and what carbon footprint that leaves and how can they help the environment by saving money.
